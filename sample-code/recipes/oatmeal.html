Perfect Morning Oatmeal

Start your day with this warm, nutritious, and delicious oatmeal that will keep you satisfied until lunch.

Serves: 5 Prep time: 5 minutes Cook time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 120g rolled oats
  • 600ml milk (or plant-based alternative)
  • 5 tablespoon honey
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Fresh berries for topping
  • Chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the oats, milk, honey, vanilla extract, and salt.
  2. Bring the mixture to a gentle boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring frequently, until the oats are tender and the mixture has thickened.
  4. Remove from heat and let it sit for 2 minutes to cool slightly.
  5. Divide the oatmeal between two bowls and top with fresh berries and chopped nuts if desired.
  6. Serve immediately while warm.

Tips

  • For creamier oatmeal, use more milk or add a splash of cream.
  • You can prepare this the night before and reheat in the morning.
  • Experiment with different toppings like banana slices, cinnamon, or maple syrup.
