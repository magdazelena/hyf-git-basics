Classic Pasta with Tomato Sauce

A simple yet delicious Italian classic that's perfect for any night of the week. This comforting dish brings together the best of Italian flavors.

Serves: 4 Prep time: 15 minutes Cook time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 400g pasta (spaghetti or penne)
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 60ml olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes to enhance the flavor.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Reduce heat to low and let the sauce simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  7. Drain the pasta, reserving 120ml of pasta water.
  8. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce along with the reserved pasta water. Toss to combine.
  9. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Tips

  • The reserved pasta water helps bind the sauce to the pasta and creates a silky texture.
  • For a richer flavor, add a splash of red wine to the sauce while it simmers.
  • Fresh herbs can be substituted for dried ones - use 3 times the amount.
